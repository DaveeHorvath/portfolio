Hive Helsinki (2023 October - )

Hive is a 42Network school based in Helsinki. They provide education to people from all types of backgrounds, teaching them how to be coders. The school this far has a 100% employment rate after completion and has alumni all over the Helsinki area. It is a self paced program, and is structured around C and C++, but the studies also include some networking and webdevelopment. The student body is also really active in other areas as well, this includes a Finland top 5 CTF team (AuroraSwarm/Nightwatchers on CTFTime) which I am part of. There was another event where I am proud of my achivements, which is HCSC which is the biggest CTF competitions in Hungary, where I got top 10 in the junior category and top 25 in the all around scoring.
